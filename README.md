# Masi's Notes

> Five things **Masi** told **Adam** one afternoon — researched, fact-checked, and answered.

Masi (Mastoureh Fathi) is a close family friend — like an aunt to Adam, a dear friend to his mum, and a quietly maternal presence in his life. Over one conversation she passed on a handful of pieces of advice and a couple of questions. Adam jotted each one into his notes app. This repo takes every one of them seriously: each note was deep-researched by a fan-out of AI agents, independently fact-checked by a separate adversarial agent, and written up as a clear brief — plus a single-page visual explainer.

## Highlights from the research

- **Spanish** genuinely unlocks ~400M people across ~18 countries and a ~$7T regional economy — with the honest caveat that the single biggest economy, **Brazil**, speaks Portuguese.
- **Universities**: the UK has heavily-subsidised routes built for exactly this — a **Knowledge Transfer Partnership** is ~67% grant-funded for SMEs and hands you a co-authored paper (the credibility Masi meant).
- **EU grants**: Masi's "10–12 countries" is the shape of a *winning* consortium, not the rule — the formal floor is just **3 entities from 3 countries**. Funding routes, IP rules, and university-spinout equity are all laid out.
- **Sofia**: why a "goose egg" lump forms in minutes and then turns into a colour-changing bruise — with a prominent **red-flags / when-to-seek-care** section. (Educational only — not a diagnosis.)
- **Tutoring**: a play-based, 30-minute session template, a personalisation engine, and an activity bank — built around the once-a-week reality that *connection beats content*.

## How it was made

Each note ran through a two-stage pipeline: a web-grounded **research agent**, then a separate **adversarial fact-checker** that re-verified every key claim. Corrections were folded back into the text — see [`METHODOLOGY.md`](METHODOLOGY.md) for the full log (e.g. the EU spinout-equity figure was updated to 2024 data, and an unsupported medical claim in Sofia's report was removed). All five briefs came back with an overall accuracy verdict of **high**.

Built with Claude Code (Opus 4.8, ultracode): **10 agents · ~100 web searches · every claim verified.**
